Geylang Night Trip

This photo trip was sparked due to one random reading i was doing on human trafficking which led to further reading with regards to legal prostitution in Singapore. Personally, felt that as long as it was a legal trade and that they were earning their own keep, there's nothing much to despise about.

Along with my fellow photobuddy, Cheese, we bought red ginberas, to give to the ladies we might see at this red light district. Hopefully, it would brighten up their night,before a long day at work.

It was a long night walk. We even had time to stop for a durian break.

The ladies who took the flowers were slightly taken aback. Probably intimidated by our cameras. However, it wasn't long before they returned a smile.

I love receiving flowers myself but the feeling of giving them out feels as good as well.

23 October 2014

Glimpse Of The Yellow Umbrella

I had a stopover in hongkong. It took me quite some time to realise that the roads were actually sealed off, due to all these gantries.

Support poured in via different forms. Not all came in the form of kicking a big hoo-hah.

Office workers who came down after knocking off and packed their dinner to eat them while sitting along the road.

Students reading their books quietly.

Speakers placing their point across to an audience without screaming their lungs out.

Different religious groups having small booths, doing their own form of praying to whoever that is up there.

Interesting sight for a tourist who had no intentions of walking in, except to see if there was a discount going on, hence the huge crowd.
